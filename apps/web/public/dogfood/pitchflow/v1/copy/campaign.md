## LinkedIn

Launching PitchFlow: a local-first, repo-native AI launch studio for developers who want the campaign to be as rigorous as the code.

PitchFlow starts from a canonical public GitHub repository, resolves a pinned commit, and gathers bounded evidence without cloning or executing the target project. GPT-5.6 Sol then works through the developer's authenticated local Codex workflow to direct one structured campaign.

That campaign can drive a static microsite, exact-size social graphics, a five-slide carousel, X and LinkedIn copy, Product Hunt copy, email, landscape and portrait videos, checksums, and a safe ZIP export.

The result is a launch narrative connected to repository evidence from intake through export.

### First comment

Hi Product Hunt — PitchFlow began with a simple premise: a developer tool's launch story should be grounded in the product's actual repository. The local workflow pins a commit, lets you inspect bounded evidence, and then asks GPT-5.6 Sol through Codex to direct the campaign as one structured system. Fresh analysis and generation remain on your machine, while the public viewer is an immutable, read-only dogfood package. The project is MIT licensed, and the source is available at https://github.com/sickn33/pitchflow.
