## LinkedIn

Introducing VibePalette 2.0.1: a Chrome and Edge extension for turning the visible tab into an export-ready color palette.

Its extraction approach combines dominance-aware RGB bins, cell winners, corner sampling, and top-band sampling—designed to keep tiny saturated details from overpowering the colors that define the page.

Choose 5–10 colors, work in HEX, RGB, or HSL, then copy the palette, create a Coolors URL, or export PNG, CSS custom properties, and JSON.

The MIT-licensed source, test suite, and release documentation are available in the repository.
